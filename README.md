**Title: Rutina - Your Daily Routine Simplified**

**Description:**
Rutina, meaning "routine" in Spanish, is the daily organization app you've been waiting for. Tired of complex applications, paywalls, and unnecessary features? We felt the same way. That's why we created Rutina, a full stack web application that combines the features you love in daily organization apps without the clutter.

**Key Features:**
- **User-Friendly Design**: Rutina offers an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making daily organization a breeze.
- **Effortless Scheduling**: Plan your day, week, and month with ease, and receive gentle reminders to stay on track.
- **Task Lists**: Create and manage task lists to ensure you never miss an important to-do item.
- **Customizable Routines**: Tailor Rutina to your unique needs without being overwhelmed by excessive features.
- **No Paywalls**: Enjoy all the features without hidden fees or subscription charges.

**Technologies Used:**
- **Frontend:** Rutina utilizes HTML, CSS, and React for a seamless user experience.
- **Backend:** Built on Express and Node.js, with PostgreSQL as the database for data storage.
- **State Management:** Redux and Saga ensure efficient state management.
- **User-Friendly Alerts:** SweetAlert enhances user interactions.
- **Date and Time Handling:** Day.js ensures accurate date and time handling.

**User Benefits:**
Rutina empowers you to take control of your daily routines without the hassle. Whether it's managing your tasks, setting up customized schedules, or staying organized, Rutina simplifies your day.

**How It Works:**
1. Sign up for a Rutina account.
2. Create your daily routines, add tasks, and set schedules.
3. Receive gentle reminders to help you stay on track.
4. Enjoy the freedom of a user-friendly organization app without any hidden fees.

**Why Rutina?**
Rutina stands out as the perfect solution for those seeking a straightforward and efficient way to manage their daily lives. Say goodbye to the clutter and hello to the simplicity of Rutina.

**Who Is It For:**
Rutina is designed for individuals who want to streamline their daily routines and stay organized, without the complexities of other apps. It's suitable for students, professionals, homemakers, and anyone looking to make the most of their day.

**Get Started:**
Ready to simplify your daily routine? Visit our website and sign up for Rutina today. Experience the power of organization without the unnecessary extras.
